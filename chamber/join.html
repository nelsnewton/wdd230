Purpose

  • Provision of easy access to valuable resources that help business save money and market
    their products.
  • Helps the government in preparing the formation of industrial law and trade.
  • To remove odd or unfair competitions by keeping the market of products static.
  • Promoting intrests of the members by influencing the govenment to reach people`s needs
    by taking claims of the members.
  • Act as representatives as those people deep in villages/ small towns can`t claim
    to the government directly.
